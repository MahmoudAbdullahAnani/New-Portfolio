[IT Trip](https://ittrip.vercel.app/) IT-Trip is a comprehensive travel booking platform that simplifies the process of reserving flights, hotels, and car rentals. Built using Next.js, React.js, Node.js, Nest.js, and MongoDB, the platform offers an intuitive and seamless experience for users, administrators, and managers. The application features multiple user roles, including User, Admin, and Manager, each with tailored dashboards for managing bookings and tracking statistics.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MahmoudAbdullahAnani/New-Portfolio/refs/heads/mainhttps://raw.githubusercontent.com/MahmoudAbdullahAnani/New-Portfolio/refs/heads/main/public/projects/ittrip.png)

## Key features include:

1. Role-Based Access: The platform supports different user roles, each with specific functionalities. Users can search and book travel services, while Admins and Managers can access detailed dashboards for operational control.
2. Dashboard & Analytics: Two separate dashboards provide real-time insightsâ€”one for statistical analysis of bookings and revenue, and another for managing and controlling the entire platform.
3. Travel Services: The platform covers a variety of services, including flight reservations, hotel bookings, and car rentals, all through an easy-to-use interface.
4. Responsive Design: The platform is fully responsive, ensuring an optimal experience on both desktop and mobile devices.
5. Secure Transactions: Users can securely book and pay for travel services using integrated payment gateways.
6. Notification System: Real-time notifications keep users updated on their bookings and upcoming trips.
